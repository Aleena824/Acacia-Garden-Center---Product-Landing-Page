Product Details

The Monstera is a popular tropical indoor plant known for its unique, large, and perforated leaves. It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The most common species, Monstera deliciosa, is sometimes called the "Swiss cheese plant" because of the distinctive holes in its leaves.

Features

Air Purifier: Known for its ability to filter indoor pollutants and improve air quality.
Distinctive Foliage: Large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that develop natural holes and splits (fenestrations) as they mature, mimicking Swiss cheese.
Easy-Care Tropical: Thrives in warm, humid conditions but is highly adaptable to average household humidity and low-maintenance care.
Toxicity: While Monstera deliciosa is not highly toxic to humans, it contains compounds called calcium oxalate crystals, which can cause irritation if ingested, especially in pets.
